Numera Sentient - $1,000,000 Secured Loan Proposal

Dear Investors,

This Letter of Intent (LOI) sets forth the indicative terms under which Numera Sentient, a research and development subsidiary of Numera Global, seeks to secure a $1,000,000 loan for a period of 24 months. The facility is intended to be utilised in support of strategic growth initiatives, ensuring optimised capital deployment and a risk-adjusted expansion.

As security for the facility, Numera Sentient shall pledge 20% equity, based on a current valuation of $7,200,000 in proprietary assets. This pledge is provided solely as collateral and does not confer upon the lender any rights of ownership, voting, management influence, or decision-making authority within the company.

Furthermore, Numera Sentient shall retain exclusive control over its proprietary know-how, intellectual property, and source code, all of which shall remain strictly protected and inaccessible to the lender under any circumstances.

This security arrangement shall remain in force for the entire loan duration and shall be released upon full repayment of all outstanding obligations.
